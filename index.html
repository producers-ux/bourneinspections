Common Questions

Everything you need to know before booking your inspection.

A standard home inspection typically takes 2–4 hours depending on the size and age of the property. Larger homes, pools, and additional structures add time. Commercial properties vary. We never rush — thoroughness is the standard, not the exception.
Reports are delivered digitally the same day, typically within a few hours of completing the inspection. The report includes photos, descriptions, and items organized by priority so you can quickly identify what requires immediate attention versus what's routine maintenance.
You're always welcome to attend, and we encourage it. Being present allows you to see findings firsthand and ask questions in real time. That said, we understand schedules don't always allow it — the report is designed to be complete and clear on its own, and we're available by phone to walk you through anything.
We inspect all accessible areas and systems: roof, foundation, structural components, electrical, plumbing, HVAC, water heater, attic, insulation, windows, doors, and exterior. Pool and spa inspections are available as an add-on. We follow InterNACHI (International Association of Certified Home Inspectors) standards of practice.
Yes. Pool and spa inspections are available as an add-on service. Given how common pools are in the Coachella Valley, we recommend adding this to any inspection where the property has a pool or spa. We evaluate equipment, plumbing, decking, safety features, and mechanical components.
A pre-listing inspection is done before you put your home on the market. It identifies issues that a buyer's inspector might find, giving you the opportunity to address them on your own terms — and your own timeline. Sellers who complete pre-listing inspections experience fewer surprises, fewer re-negotiations, and faster closings.
Yes. We offer an investor retainer program designed for buyers who are acquiring properties regularly. This includes priority scheduling, consistent report formatting for lender use, and volume pricing. If you're building a portfolio in the Coachella Valley or high desert, contact us to discuss a program built around your needs.
Use the contact form below, call us directly, or email. We respond quickly and accommodate short escrow windows. Once booked, we'll confirm all details with you and your agent.
